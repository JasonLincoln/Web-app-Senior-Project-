Welcome to SkillSwap!
SkillSwap is a website where students can learn from tutors about specific subjects and for tutors to teach their expertise to others. Learn more about SkillSwap by continuing to read about us below!

Featured

SkillSwap contains many features available to you to the start for free! Our slideshow below shows some of the features you can use as a student and/or tutor!

Create tutoring sessions, or receive them!

Creating a tutoring session is easy regardless of if you're the student or the tutor.

Search for tutors!

Our explore page makes it easy to find tutors. Tutors themselves give background information, and the skills they're proficient at, as well as their ratings!

Message others!

Message others, especially tutors, about sessions or questions about your subjects!

How does SkillSwap Work?

Learn from other students!

Learn about the skills you want through tutors with expertise on the field. SkillSwap features a rating system that shows the ratings of tutors, allowing you to choose from credible and trustworthy instructors.

Track your progress and progress quickly!

SkillSwap tracks your progress right where you left off, along with a solid recommendation of tutors to choose from.

Teach others about your skills!

Interested in being a tutor? It is easy to become one when you register an account for SkillSwap! Teach other students your skills with an easy way of creating a course for others to learn!

Frequently Asked Questions

You can use SkillSwap at no cost! All tutors here volunteer to spend their time teaching subjects they are experienced at. If you would like to reward them for their services, you would do so on an external website.
To get started with SkillSwap, you can go to the explore section in the navigation bar and select the category that the skill you want to learn falls under. You can find what each skill category contains by hovering over the cards that are above the FAQ section. You will be able to message the tutor and ask for a session. This page will also allow you to search for certain tutors.
To edit your profile, click on the profile icon and click on the profile button. There will be a gear icon that will be aside the profile image. If you are a tutor, it is important to be descriptive in your biography in order to appeal to students. If you are a student, it might be best to describe background information or specific aspects of the subjects you need to learn.
To find the list of sessions you request or schedule, click on the sessions button in the navigation bar or here. In the page, you will find upcoming sessions, requested sessions, and previous sessions you've used. This page can also be used to request a session to a tutor.
